Use this screen to actually perform the request of the inspection. You can override the “Requested By” field and “Phone Number” with another name and phone number. If overridden, then both the registered user and the new name/number will be contacted via SMS or email. Select your preference of either SMS (text), email, or both. Comments may also be entered which will be seen by the clerk and inspector.

Use "Submit" button to send the request for inspection. You will be contacted by your chosen method when the request is actually scheduled by the inspector the day of inspection.
