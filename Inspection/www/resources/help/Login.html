Welcome to the City of Surrey Mobile Inspection Request app for your mobile device.

In order to login, you must first register with the City of Surrey Planning and Development front counter.

Once registered, you will be able to login and use the application to view your permits and to request inspections from your mobile device.

You can use the info button, to access the settings menu if you would like to customize the app.


FAQs


What is the a.m. / p.m. schedule phone number?

604-591-4650


What does a.m. / p.m. inspection time mean?

A.M. inspections are preformed between 9:15 a.m. to 11:59 am. More detailed inspection times are currently unavailable.

P.M. inspections are preformed between 12:00 p.m. to 4:00 pm. More detailed inspection times are currently unavailable.


Are inspections on the a.m. / p.m. line in order of inspection?

No. Many areas have more than one inspector. The calls are divided by a.m. and p.m. only.


How do I cancel a requested inspection?

Call 604-591-4231 and hold on the line to speak directly to a Building Inspections Clerk. Cancellations left on the phone line or web inspections will not be processed.


Is there a cut off time for cancelling a requested inspection?

Cancellations cannot be received after 9:00 a.m. due to computerization of inspection bookings.


Can I call in for future inspections?

Please call after 3:00 p.m. the day prior to requested inspection date to book your inspection.


What is the cut off time for booking inspections?

3:00 p.m. for the next working day.


What is my area number?

Your construction site is located within 6 building inspection areas in Surrey. There can be more than one Building Inspector working in each area number.


Where do I find my area number on my permit?

The area number is located on the top right hand corner of the building permit under the permit number.


What does conditional approval mean?

Conditional approval is an approval that has been granted with deficiencies. Construction is approved to continue and any deficiencies listed will be carried forward and inspected at the next building inspection stage.


What does deferred mean?

Deferred means that your inspection will not be conducted on the requested date. The inspection will be automatically carried forward to the next business day. You do not need to rebook a deferred inspection.


How do I find if my inspection is deferred?

You will be contacted by the Building Inspection Clerk if your inspection is deferred to the next business day. Please be sure to leave a phone number that you can be reached at.


Is there first come, first serve for calling in inspections?

No. All inspections that are called in before the 3:00 p.m. cut of time will be put in the for the next business day.


What is the schedule line phone number to book an inspection?

604-591-4231


Can I request a specific time for an inspection?

No. Currently the inspections are booked for the next business day by the Building Inspection Clerks. On the day of the requested inspection the Building Inspectors will arrange their areas by either a.m. or p.m.


What is required to book an inspection?

Please provide your building permit number, type of inspection; site address of inspection; a contact name and phone number. Any requests missing information will not be processed.


How do I book a draintile inspection?

Draintile is an inspection that is performed by the Plumbing Inspection Department.


What is the number to book draintile inspections?

604-591-4245.
