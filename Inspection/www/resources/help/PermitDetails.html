The Case Details screen gives you a detailed look at the selected case. The top section contains the Folder/Case Number, address, and other information about the case.

The Bottom section shows the list of available inspections which may be requested. Simply select the inspection you wish to request, and select “Request Inspection” from the menu. Once requested, you will see the Requested Date field update to indicate that the request is now scheduled.

From this screen, you can also view your inspection result for a selected inspection.
